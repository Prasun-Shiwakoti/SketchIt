GAME DESCRIPTION:

SketchIt is an online multiplayer game which you can play with you friends as well. Here one of the player in the room will be assigned as an artist and will be provided with a random word which he/she has to explain to the the others players in the room in 90 seconds. Then all the players who are guessing the word can input your guess in the chat box and if you get the word correct then you will be rewarded with 100 points. Finally after 5 rounds the player with the maximum points wins.
